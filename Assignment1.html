My Summer Adventures

My Trip To California

It was a long, quick trip. We loaded our vehicle and headed to Northern California for the 4th of July weekend for a family reunin. We then made haste to Magic Moutain. By the time we returned home, we had traveled some 1300 miles in three and one half days.

My Trip To The Rockies

After a year's preparation, it was time to go backpacking with the Boy Scouts. We flew to Colorado Springs, toured the Air Force Acadamy, Pike's Peak, Garden of the Gods, and the Olympic training center. We chartered a bus to the Philmont Scout Ranch in Norther New Mexico and began our 60 miles backpack trek. Afterwards, we boarded Amtrack's and rode the rails to Kingman, and then chartered a bus home.

My Trip To The Mid West

My wife and I drove our son's little red Ford Mustang some 2900 miles to him in Indiana where he attends college.In route, we stopped at Nauvoo, Illinois and Detroit, Michigan.We then boarded Amtrak in Chicage, and rode it to Salk Lake City, where we boarded a bus which returned us to Las Vegas.
